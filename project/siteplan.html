Site Name: DreamHome Listings

DreamHome was given to this site because it is a site where users can find their DreamHome.

Site Purpose:

The website serves as a platform to showcase real estate properties for sale, providing an interactive browsing experience for potential buyers while capturing user information for engagement.

Color Scheme:

  • Primary Color: Dark Desaturated Blue (#2C3E50) – Represents trust and professionalism. To be Used header and footer backgrouds
  • Secondary Color: White (#FFFFFF) – Ensures clarity and contrast. Used for text
  • Background Color: Light Gray (#F8F9FA) – Provides a modern and clean look.

Typography:

  • Headings: Montserrat, sans-serif (Bold and modern)
  • Body Text: Open Sans, sans-serif (Easy to read and professional)
  • Buttons and Highlights: Roboto, sans-serif (Clear and engaging)

Scenarios:

  • A user searching for available real estate properties based on location or price range.
  • A buyer selecting and saving properties for later review.
  • A potential buyer submitting their contact details for follow-up.

Home Page Layout (Desktop View)

  • Header: Logo on the left; navigation links (Home, Catalog, Contact) on the right.
  • Hero Section: A prominent banner (Hero image) with a tagline and call-to-action button.
  • Featured Properties: Dynamically loaded properties displayed in a grid format.
  • Footer: Contact information, social media links, and a newsletter subscription form.

Home Page Layout (Mobile View)

  • Header: Collapsible menu with logo centered.
  • Hero Section: Full-width hero image with tagline and call-to-action button.
  • Featured Properties: Single-column stacked property listings for easy scrolling.
  • Footer: Stacked layout with contact links and subscription form.
